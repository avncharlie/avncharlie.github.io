Arctic Monkeys - the British Indie rockers who became U.K's biggest band of the millenium.

From the moment the Arctic Monkeys released ‘Whatever People Say I Am, That’s What I’m Not’ in 2006, they’ve been one of the most buzzed-about bands on the planet. By distilling the sounds of Franz Ferdinand, the Clash, the Strokes and the Libertines into a hybrid of swaggering indie rock and danceable neo punk, the Arctic Monkeys have  been a phenomenal success.
