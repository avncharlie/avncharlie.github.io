Battery saver plugin. Never run out battery again.

A battery saver plugin that can control the brightness, Wifi, Bluetooth, and the Mac keyboard backlight, straight from Spotlight. Set presets for what controls are turned on and off when the battery saver is activated. If you're still not amazed, battery saver can even remember how your Mac was, so you can pick right back up whenever you want to.
