GET INVOLVED

You could make a difference in the lives of this destitute women and children by partnering with us in various ways. We need prayer support for every area of our work, for all Mukti residents, and all staff and volunteers who dedicate their time and talents for this amazing work.

Financial assistance

You could partner with us in this life giving ministry by providing financial assistance. We handle all donations with accountability and transparency so you can be assured that your contributions are making make a difference.

Personal involvement

You can get involved in this ministry in a more personal way by being a short term volunteer at our campus in Kedgaon. This could be organised as individuals, as a family or as small groups. We could use your talents to help us in our day to day work, while you get a much better understanding of Mukti at ground level.

Get involved with an international branch of Mukti

If you live outside India you can also get involved with one of our overseas partners who support our mission. You could also become a local ambassador for this great cause and help generate awareness and support from your local community or church.
