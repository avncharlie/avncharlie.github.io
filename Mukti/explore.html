Woman's shelter

Mukti works tirelessly to restore dignity and self worth for destitute women and children in a caring, compassionate and loving family environment, following the mandate and vision of Pandita Ramabai.

Schools

Our schools endeavour to educate and empower hundreds of children every year providing free education in both English and Marathi medium, aiming for a better hope and future for every child.

Hospital

Our onsite hospital has been providing free medical care for Mukti residents and the local community for several decades.

Church

The campus has a century old church built by Pandita Ramabai, where church services are still held every week for Mukti residents and the local community.

Farm

Kitchen

Mukti's kitchen staff is always hard at work, producing nutritious food for over six hundred people every day.

Computer academy
